Stay Awake is out now!

  • August 21st, 2025
  • Ace Ambrose

There's always something hiding in the dark. Be careful, insomniac. Stay calm, stay vigliant, and most importantly: Stay. Awake.

After ages of working on it, Scarlet and I are finally finished with our latest album, Stay Awake. Five songs of our musical prowess, perfect for when the clock strikes midnight, and the sounds of whatever's lurking in the shadows, ready to strike, has you reaching for a weapon to fend off the beast.

This album takes inspiration from the late hours of one's childhood, and the way we all would hide under the covers when we thought we heard or saw something strange, with the illusion of safety. It also takes inspiration from the idea of insomnia, particularly the kind where you avoid sleeping in order to dodge nightmares.

The album is out for digital purchase on iTunes and Bandcamp. Additionally, you can stream it on Spotify, Amazon Music, Apple Music, and YouTube Music. Happy listening, adventurer.
