About Us

Who Are We?

We're Stage Fright, a punk rock band based in Columbus looking to change the world with the power of music! In our albums that walk the line between your typical hard rock and metalcore genres, we tackle the harsh realities of society through the lens of the monsters woven into folklore that hide in the dark.

How We Met

On a brisk Halloween morning back in 2021, the five gathered in Ace's apartment, character sheets and dice in hand, ready to embark on the adventure that would soon shape their futures. At the time, it was just a fun D&D one shot, written and dungeon-mastered by Ace, with the purpose of giving his friends something to do when their usual spooky plans were cancelled. During one of the many breaks in the campaign, Cora mentioned that she played bass guitar, and from there, the band was born! Each member's character became their stage personas, with Ace pulling a character from one of his previous homebrew campaigns. Taking it a step further, they created cosplays to match, donning them for performances and official band work.

Ace Ambrose

26, Keyboard

Ace Ambrose is the band's clever faerie, weaving together ideas and music in ways that leave fans wanting more. A storyteller by nature, Ace works with Scarlet to turn concepts to verses and verses to full songs. When not writing or playing for the band, he's writing novels and D&D campaigns. 25, Guitarist

River Wylde is the band's sneaky goblin, creating riffs to remember for decades to come. In addition to being their guitar, he's come up with and designed most of the wearable merch, with Evie filling in with art here and there. In his spare time, he enjoys playing pranks on his friends, carrying in online games, and decimating them in Super Smash Bros Ultimate (he mains Inkling with the blue alternate costume).

Cora Astrite

26, Bass Guitarist

Cora Astrite is the band's gorgeous gorgon, laying down thrilling bass lines that create the perfect vibe for any song they play. In addition to modeling all the merch prototypes for Evie and River, she creates the band's videos; whether that be music videos, new album trailers, or announcements for tours. In her free time, she does contemporary dance and studies video and film editing. She also enjoys cosplaying and going to conventions for her favorite media.

Evie Nightshade

23, Drummer

Evie Nightshade is the band's spunky werewolf, forging drum fills that take the music to a whole new level. In addition to being the pulse of the group, she's the artist for all the album covers and merch, taking River's ideas for the latter and making them a reality. In addition to drawing for the band, she does art in her spare time, making fanart for her favorite shows, games, and movies. She also enjoys reading.
