NEW MERCH DROP: Insomniac Line!

  • August 23rd, 2025
  • River Wylde

In celebration of our latest album, Stay Awake, going live, we've created a new line of fashionable merch to show your dedication!

Introducing the Insomniac Line, the perfect thing to wear on those late nights when there's something keeping you up, and perfect for those drowsy mornings when sleep refuses to stop clinging to your mind. Featuring three clothing options and the vinyls for every Stage Fright album, you'll be well-prepared for whatever you decide to do with your twilight hours

As per usual, the clothes are made with high-quality, ethically-sourced materials, and are built to last. In addition, we've perfected the comfy factor. Heck, Cora even slept in the hoodie we made as part of this!

And on top of it all, it won't make your wallet cry. Some bands sell their merch at ridiculous prices, just because they know you want something with your favorite artists on it. We, however, know that life is expensive enough as is. So we've worked hard to make sure that you won't have to make a deal with the fae and sell away your firstborn just to afford some comfy clothes.

Everything's available for pre-order right now up until October, and if you buy ahead of time, you can choose to get a free copy of the vinyl with your purchase! Can't order just yet? Not to worry! They'll still be available for general purchase after the pre-order dates close. Head on over to the merch tab to place an order!

That's all for now, adventurers!
