The Nightmares Tour!

  • September 5th, 2025
  • Evie Nightshade

After what feels like an eternity only doing smaller gigs, now comes our biggest tour yet! Will we see you there?

Hey adventurers! We've been gone for a bit, haven't we? No need to worry though, because now that some things behind the scenes are done, we'll be going on tour!

The Nightmares Tour features five locations across the country, and runs from October 22nd to Halloween, returning to the roots of how the band was formed. Bring your charms and your costumes, because we're going all out! It'll be the Halloween party of a century!

If you're interested in accepting the invitation to our rebellious revel, head on over to tours to find a location near you, and order tickets now! And if there aren't locations near you, worry not! If the Nightmares Tour does well, we'll expand even further!

That's all for now, adventurers! Hope to see you there!
